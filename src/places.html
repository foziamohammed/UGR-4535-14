Santorini, Greece Santorini is an island in the southern Aegean Sea, southeast of Greece's mainland. Populated as early as 14th century BC, this small village is now a popular tourist attraction. This is because of the many beautiful houses built into the cliffs along the coastline, along with gorgeous blue waters surrounding the island. The beautiful site is considered to be one the most beautiful places to visit in Greece, for good reason.
